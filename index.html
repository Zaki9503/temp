What is Global Warming?

Global warming refers to the long-term rise in Earth's average surface temperature due to human activities, primarily the emission of greenhouse gases. It has far-reaching effects on the planet, including melting ice caps, rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and loss of biodiversity.

Why It Matters

Our actions today will determine the future of our planet. By learning about global warming and taking steps to reduce our carbon footprint, we can mitigate its impact and protect future generations.
